About Our Resort

Hidden in the lap of nature near Chitri Dam, Kolhapur, Jungle Spring Adventure Resort is a place where adventure, relaxation, and togetherness come naturally. Surrounded by greenery and peaceful dam views, our resort is designed for families, couples, and groups looking for a refreshing break from busy city life.We offer a perfect mix of adventure activities like Zipline and Sky Cycle, a clean and relaxing swimming pool, and comfortable cottages with beautiful dam views. Whether it’s a fun-filled family trip, a short weekend getaway, or a group outing, our resort has something for everyone.If you’re searching for a family-friendly adventure resort in Kolhapur near Chitri Dam, Jungle Spring Adventure Resort welcomes you to experience nature, adventure, and memorable moments—all in one place.

Our Rooms & Cottages

Deluxe Rooms (Non-AC)

Perfect for guests who enjoy natural weather and scenic views. These non-air-conditioned rooms offer beautiful views of the mountains, lake, and swimming pool, allowing you to wake up to fresh air and soothing natural landscapes. The rooms are well-ventilated, comfortable, and ideal for a relaxed stay.

Best for: Nature lovers and budget-friendly travelers

Premium Cottages (AC)

Designed for guests who want comfort along with uninterrupted views. These fully air-conditioned cottages feature mountain and lake touch views, with large glass windows that let you enjoy the beauty of nature right from inside your room. The interiors are elegant, spacious, and perfect for couples and families.

Best for: Couples and comfort seekers

Luxury Premium Cottages (Lake View)

Offer an exclusive and unforgettable experience. Located inside the lake, these cottages provide a breathtaking dam view with water on three sides. The interiors are designed with glass walls, allowing you to enjoy panoramic views of the lake from inside the cottage itself. This category is ideal for guests seeking privacy, romance, and luxury.

Best for: Honeymooners and luxury stay experiences
